About the Documentary

Synopsis

Trinidad and Tobago’s undeniable passion for cars has earned it the title of the Caribbean’s automotive hub. This documentary explores the island’s vibrant car culture through discussions with enthusiasts, including my cousin, Kyle Legendre—an automotive mechanic and project car owner. The film takes a deep dive into the perspectives of a drag racer, a drop-low specialist, a fabricator, a garage owner, a street racer, and two off-roaders. Each will share their journey, the challenges of car ownership, and the financial dedication behind their passion.

Logline

Trinidad and Tobago’s car culture is a passion-driven community fueled by skill, speed, and dedication.
